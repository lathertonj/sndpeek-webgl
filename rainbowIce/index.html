Rainbow Ice

Welcome to Rainbow Ice, a lactose-free dessert!

This application is a sonic visualizer that displays information related to the time domain, the frequency domain, the spectral centroid, and the energy in the signal.


Usage:

i:

toggles the inner ice rings, which display past FFTs

c:

toggles the inner cone lines, which duplicate the waveform

m: 

toggles the meltyness, which is controlled by a slewed moving average of the spectral centroid

b:

toggles the booms, which emanate from your rainbow ice on frames that have enough energy

r:

toggles drawing the booms in random locations if there is more than one
